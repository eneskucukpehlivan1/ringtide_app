🌀 RINGTIDE

Privacy Policy · Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Ringtide ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free arcade game developed by an independent developer. This privacy policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information ourselves. The app does not require account registration or login.

All game progress (score, streak, themes, badges) is stored locally on your device using device storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers because we do not operate any servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data to serve ads, including:

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
  • Device information and approximate location
  • Interaction data (e.g. ad clicks)

Google's privacy policy applies to data collected by AdMob: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Advertising Consent (GDPR / ATT)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the app will request your consent before loading personalized ads via Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP). You may decline, in which case non-personalized ads will be shown instead.

On iOS, the app may also request permission to track your activity across apps and websites via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You may deny this permission at any time in your device Settings.

5. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

All local data can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app. Because we hold no server-side data, there is nothing further to request deletion of from us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact
